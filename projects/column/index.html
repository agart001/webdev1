Section 1 - A Sad Reunion

On April 6, 1972, two men – Guy Burmieux, a worker at Joint Français, and Jean-Yvon Antignac, a riot policeman. The two recognized eachother, they were childhood friends.

Section 2 - Hairs on End

On August 20, 1975, at Moro Rock in California’s Sequoia National Park, two brothers, Michael and Sean McQuilken, were struck by lightning. This picture was taken moments before hand.

Section 3 - Big Booms

On June 5, 1952, Harold Edgerton an MIT physicist and photographer, took a picture of a nuclear detonation at the Nevada ProvingGrounds by Edgerton.
